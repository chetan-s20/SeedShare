# 🌓 Quick Start Guide: Using Dark/Light Mode Toggle

## Where to Find It

Look at the **top-right corner** of the navigation bar:
- Next to the login/signup buttons (when logged out)
- Next to your profile picture (when logged in)

## How to Use

### Step 1: Click the Theme Icon
- You'll see a **sun icon** ☀️ in light mode
- Or a **moon icon** 🌙 in dark mode

### Step 2: Choose Your Theme
A dropdown menu will appear with three options:
1. **Light** ☀️ - Bright, white background
2. **Dark** 🌙 - Dark background, easy on the eyes
3. **System** 💻 - Matches your computer's theme

### Step 3: Enjoy!
Your choice is saved automatically and will be remembered next time you visit!

## Tips

### For Night-Time Use
- Switch to **Dark Mode** for reduced eye strain
- Perfect for browsing before bed
- Battery-friendly on OLED screens

### For Daytime Use
- **Light Mode** offers better contrast in bright environments
- Easier to read in sunlight
- Traditional, familiar look

### For Automatic Switching
- Choose **System** to match your OS
- Changes automatically at sunrise/sunset (if your OS is configured)
- Best of both worlds!

## What Changes in Dark Mode?

- ✅ Background becomes dark
- ✅ Text becomes light
- ✅ Cards get darker with lighter borders
- ✅ All buttons adapt colors
- ✅ Forms and inputs get dark styling
- ✅ Community posts are easy to read
- ✅ Images and icons maintain contrast

## Troubleshooting

### Theme Not Saving?
- Check if cookies/localStorage are enabled
- Try clearing your browser cache

### Flickering on Load?
- This is normal for first-time visitors
- Subsequent visits will be smooth

### Some Elements Look Wrong?
- Try refreshing the page
- Report any issues to our team

## Browser Compatibility

✅ Works on all modern browsers:
- Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari
- Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile)
